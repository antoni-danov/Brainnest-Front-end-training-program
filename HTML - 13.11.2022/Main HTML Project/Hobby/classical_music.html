My story

I am a professional flute player. I started my journey at age of five and followed my studies in the National School of Music in Sofia, Bulgaria.

After that I was addmited in the National Conservatory of Music - Sofia. Through the years, I participated actively in many concerts, ceremonies, competitions and events and I was part of differents orchestras.

In 2018, after a short break due to changes in place of living I finished my Master degree and in 2019 I turned to my second passion in life the programming. Since, the classical music is transformed in a hobby for the moment.
